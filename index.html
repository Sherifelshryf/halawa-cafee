Our Heritage

A Taste of Authentic Egypt

Halawa Café was born from a simple love — the kind shared over a morning table, the smell of freshly baked bread, the ritual of a perfectly seasoned ful. We brought that intimacy to you.

Every dish on our menu is crafted with ingredients sourced locally and recipes passed down through generations. We don't reinvent tradition — we honour it.

Why Halawa

The Halawa Difference

We believe breakfast is the most sacred meal of the day. That's why we do it — and only it — with uncompromising intention.

  • Every ful recipe made in-house, seasoned by hand each morning
  • Freshly baked feteer using traditional Egyptian stone-milling
  • Locally sourced, seasonal ingredients — zero compromise on quality
  • A warm, unhurried atmosphere perfect for long morning conversations
  • Full breakfast packages for families and groups
